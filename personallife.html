Personal Life

I believe that a good balance is essential to living life, in order to keep myself at peace and in a state of equilibrium, I like to do things such as:

  • Reading
    • I primarily read non-fiction, with a bit of fiction for leisure/relaxation. Some of the genres that I read include:
    • Stoic Philosophy, thoroughly enjoy reading the discourses of Marcus Aurelius, Seneca, and Epictetus.
    • Psychology, books such as The Person and The Situation by Lee Ross and Richard Nisbett, and Consciousness Explained by Daniel C. Dennett.
    • Self-Help, books such as Think & Grow Rich by Napoleon Hill, and Think Like A Winner by Dr. Walter Doyle Staples.
    • Investment Philosophy, books such as Zero To One by Peter Thiel, and Against The Gods by Peter L. Bernstein.
  • Soccer
    • I've played soccer since I was 8 years old, still do to this day. Soccer has allowed me to keep in healthy shape and compete in high ranking tournaments across Canada.
    • U12 Provincial Finalist in Medicine Hat, was Tournament Top Scorer. (2017)
    • U15 Provincial Finalist in Medicine Hat. (2019)
    • U17 Surrey SX Showcase Tournament. (2021)
  • Listening To Music
    • I've been able to play the piano and guitar from a young age and knowing how to do so has allowed me to develop a strong passion towards good music, some of the music that I listen to includes:
    • Piano music, such as the type composed by Ludivico Einaudi, Yann Tiersen, and Joep Beving.
    • Jazz music, the type by Frank Sinatra, Dean Martin and Bobby Darin.
    • Classic Italian music, artists such as Toto Cutugno, Adriano Celentano, Eros Ramazzotti, Ricchi E Poveri and Andrea Bocelli.
  • Spending time in Moldova
    • Moldova (My Home Country) allows me to relax and be my whole self, some of the things I enjoy doing there include:
    • Spending time with family; Growing up I have always placed key value on the importance of family and being in Moldova allows me to be beside everyone I hold close.
    • Spending time in nature; Moldova has a humongous amount of untouched nature and this allows me to spend a lot of time going on walks, relaxing in nature, and exploring.
    • Partaking in the culture; Moldova and it's inhabitants place a key value on culture and because of this I always have something to do, from Folk Culture, to Religious holidays,
      there is always something bringing the people together.
